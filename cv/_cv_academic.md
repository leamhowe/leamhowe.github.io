## Fieldwork Experience

During my PhD, I have completed fieldwork in the Cairngorms, Scotland, to collect spectal reflective measurements and perform GNSS mapping of late-lying snow patches. My first field season (2023) we used the handheld ASD Field Spectrometer. The second season I qualified as a drone pilot and used the DJI M350 drone with a MAIA multispectral sensor matched to Sentinel-2. 

I have also assisted the Airborne Research and Innovation (ARI) team at the University of Edinburgh with fieldwork as part of the [Forest and Peatlands project](https://sustainability.ed.ac.uk/operations/forest-peatland) aimed to restore peatlands and expand forests in Scotland.
